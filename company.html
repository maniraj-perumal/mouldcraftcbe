WHO WE ARE

Your Trusted Manufacturing Partner in Coimbatore.

Our Expertise & Experience

At Mould Craft, we bring together over 25 years of combined expertise to serve the diverse needs of manufacturing industries. Based in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, India, our dedicated team specializes in providing comprehensive solutions, from initial prototyping to high-quality finished products.

Our core competencies include:

  • Prototyping: Turning your concepts into tangible models.
  • Aluminium Casting: Delivering robust and precise aluminium components.
  • Machining: Offering high-accuracy machining services.
  • Gravity Die Casting: Providing reliable and cost-effective casting solutions.

Our Facilities

At Mould Craft, our state-of-the-art facility in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, is designed to support the complete manufacturing lifecycle — from concept to delivery. We blend advanced equipment, skilled professionals, and smart workflows to deliver precision and performance at every stage.

Foundry Operations

Advanced melting and molding systems for aluminum and non-ferrous alloys — ensuring high strength and precise dimensional control.

Spectrum Analysis

On-site spectrometer confirms alloy accuracy by verifying chemical makeup and maintaining material consistency.

Heat Treatment

Carefully managed heating cycles enhance hardness, strength, and wear resistance for a wide range of applications.

Shot Blasting

High-pressure abrasive blasting cleans surfaces effectively — improving both appearance and coating adhesion.

Machine Shop Access

Integrated support from local machining partners allows fast, efficient conversion from raw casting to final product.

Quality from Single Piece to Large Volumes

Whether you require a single prototype or large-scale production runs, Mould Craft is equipped to handle your needs with unwavering commitment to quality. Our processes are designed to ensure exceptional results from the initial design phase to the final finished product.
